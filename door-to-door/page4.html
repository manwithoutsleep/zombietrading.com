Script for World-Wide Door to Door Ministries

  1. Hi, my name is __________________ and I am out Evangelizing for the day. Could I have five minutes of your time?
  2. I would like to share a brief message with you for the day.
  3. Read a Parable… Suggested: Parable of the Workers in the Vineyard from Matthew 20:1-16
  4. I would like to share my Testimony with you… Hand them your written Testimony.
  5. Invite them to church, with either a business card of the church, a business card of the Pastor of the church and/or a brochure of the church, with times for Bible study and church services.
  6. Have you ever received Jesus Christ or would like to?
  7. Do you have any prayer requests? Pray for them on the spot or tell them that you will pray for them at a corporate prayer service.
  8. Closing: Thank you for your time and may God richly bless you!
