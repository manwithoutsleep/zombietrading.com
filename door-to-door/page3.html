What we are trying to accomplish at
World Wide Door to Door Ministries!

Luke 10:2

Therefore said he unto them, The harvest truly is great, but the labourers are few: pray ye therefore the Lord of the harvest, that he would send forth labourers into his harvest.
We at World Wide Door to Door Ministries (W.W.D.T.D.M.) have our sights set on quantity of contacts. We believe we plant a seed & the Holy Spirit produces the harvest. Our objective is to save souls, it is not to see if we can necessarily get the prospective soul to your Church, but to become part of the Body of Jesus Christ. Most Church evangelical outreaches deal with on the spot conversion… or at least that is the objective. That is not the case with World Wide Door to Door Ministries; we deal with the philosophy of Johnny Appleseed: Plant the seed & move on! My point is to have a 5-7-minute format. If it goes over, so what? We count on prayer support & the powers of the Holy Spirit to guide us to the proper people & at least a 5-minute occasion to get a blessing from the witness to the prospective soul. It's the story of the Mustard Seed Matthew 13:31-32; it's all about FAITH in Jesus that grows to be a large tree. However, it starts out as the tiniest seed known & grows & grows! You never know where that next Billy Graham is going to come from, but you do know this much: That seed came from somewhere.
