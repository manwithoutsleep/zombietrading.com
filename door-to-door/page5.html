World Wide Door to Door Ministries

When going Door to Door and Handing out TESTIMONIES, you just might be contacted... then what? I call these the A, B, C's of DOOR TO DOOR contacts. These contacts will usually fall into 3 different/separate catagories or the A, B, C's of DOOR TO DOOR contacts.

The A, B, C's of World Wide DOOR TO DOOR Ministries contacts:

Type A---The Willing

They are the HOT AND READY FOR SERVICE! These are the contacts who go and get straight into the flock of sheep, under the direction of the Pastor or Hierarchy of the Church. These are good sheep in the Shepard's flock and listen to the Pastor and Hierarchy closely. Thus; can grow to be terrific assets to the Church!

Type B---The Lost

These are the people who contact you from your Testimony who need time and love, an open ear and advice. Who are ready for the good life of Christianity, but don't know how to get there! They also may need Exodus 20:1-17 and Luke 10:27 through Galatians 3:24.

Type C---The Rebellious

Be careful not to spend too much time with these types. Chances are they will never change. They can be time stealers who take your valuable time, that you could be using for more World Wide Door to Door Ministries activities and planting more seeds for the Church and Jesus! All they basically know is Satan! Pray, pray and more prayer is usually the best thing you can do for them. They don't know the difference between Heaven and Hell, to them there is no difference! They desperately need Exodus 20:1-17 and Luke 10:27 through Galatians 3:24. These rebels are red flag types... caution, caution, caution!!!

So regardless: if it is Type A (THE WILLING), Type B (THE LOST), Type C (THE REBELLIOUS)...the World Wide Door To Door Ministry march still goes on and Your Ministry continues! If it is only 3-6 contacts in a day/evening or 25-50 keep on moving on. The search regardless; if it is to fill up every seat in the Church or find that next Billy Graham, the seed has been planted! Move on and plant another seed for your Church and the Kingdom of Jesus Christ!
